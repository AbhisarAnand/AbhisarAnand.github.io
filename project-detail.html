On-Time Delivery Compliance Dashboard

Role Data Analyst Intern
Company Brooks Automation
Tech Stack Python, Streamlit, NLP

The Challenge

Global supplier performance was difficult to track in real-time. The sourcing team needed a way to visualize OTD (On-Time Delivery) metrics and understand the "why" behind delays without manually sifting through thousands of emails and news reports.

The Solution

I designed and developed an AI-powered intelligence dashboard. It aggregates data from the ERP system to provide part-level insights. Key features include:

  • Automated web scraping to fetch news related to specific suppliers.
  • A custom AI chatbot that allows directors to query the data using natural language.
  • Real-time Purchase Price Variance (PPV) detection.

Impact

The solution was demoed to the CIO and Strategic Sourcing Directors, driving visibility and credibility. It is now being used to proactively manage supplier risks and improve global sourcing efficiency.
